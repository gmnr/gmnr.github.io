Quirky and fun, just like the `xkcd` comic strip, the book is a collection of
reader submitted question that the author tries to answer using his scientific
knowledge.

It is a very light read, with most questions not taking more than 3/4 pages. You
can comfortably skim it, without being anchored by a story that develops.

All in all is a very fun read that scratches a deeper itch of its comic strip
counterpart.
